CVA/TIA checklist

Diagnostic tests

  • CT head (noncontrast)
  • CT brain perfusion
  • CT angio head and neck
  • MRI brain without gadolinium
  • EKG
  • TTE with agitated saline (bubble study)
  • cardiac monitor/telemetry

Medical management

  • aspirin
  • high-dose statin
  • diabetes management
